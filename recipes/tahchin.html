Tahchin

Tahchin is a traditional Persian rice dish made with saffron-infused rice, yogurt, eggs, and often chicken or lamb, and in this case with stinkging nettles too.
It is layered and baked until there is a golden, crispy crust on the outside called tahdig.
The dish is usually served with barberries, pistachios, or almonds but I don't decorate it. Flipping it is spectacular enough.

Ingredients

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 2 cups Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp saffron (bloomed w a bit of hot/warm water)
  • Salt (3/4 tbsp / cup of rice????)/ pepper
  • 2 cup rice (parboiled)
  • 2 Medium Carrots
  • 1 Medium yellow onion
